Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ section is a section of the website that informs the customers about the products and services offered by the website. FAQ section also help our customer to ask any kind of questions related to their purchase or services.

  • How to buy our product?

    Step 1: Select the product you want to buy by clicking the buy it now button in product page then click check out.
    Step 2: Select the payment method you prefer.
    Step 3: Fill in the order form with all your details.
    Step 4: Click “Submit Order” button to confirm your order and make payment.
    Step 5: Our team will check and process your order as soon as possible once we received it.

  • Do I need to purchase the Operation system, HDMI cable, wire, cooling paste, CPU fan, keyboard, mouse, screen, earphone, speaker and other accessories by myself when purchasing a complete host?

    If you have purchased a genuine Windows license, we will install the corresponding Windows version for you, install the driver and format the hard disk.

    HDMI cable and power cable:
    As long as you purchase a complete set of computers, we will confirm with you whether you need to provide HDMI cables and power cables, and they will be provided free of charge if necessary.

    Processor Cooling:
    If your original boxed ("Box") processor came with a cooler, we'll install it for you.
    If you choose a packaged processor ("Tray"), we will compare the specifications of the boxed processor. If the same boxed CPU comes with a heat sink and you have not purchased a heat sink, we will provide a heat sink and install it for you.

    Regardless of whether the CPU cooler is purchased separately, thermal grease is attached, so you don’t need to pay for additional thermal grease when you buy a whole host.
    However, some CPU models do not come with a CPU fan, and customer service will confirm with you whether it is necessary to repurchase.

    Other:
    Peripheral products such as keyboard, mouse, screen, earphones and speakers need to be purchased separately.
